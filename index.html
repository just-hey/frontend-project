Know Your Show

Welcome to Know Your Show! A short quiz to test your ear and knowledge.

How to play:

  • Click the "Play" button to listen to an Opening theme of a TV show.
  • Once you think you know the correct show the song belongs to make your selection and you'll be moved to the next show/song.
  • At the end you'll get to see how you did and your score will be saved in your browser's Local Storage.

    • Hope you enjoy!
